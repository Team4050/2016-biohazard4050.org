I walked through the shadows of the wood, searching for a sliver of light. Home was through the wood, but I had lost my way by the broken lamp post. I slid my hand along the bark of the trees, wishing for some form of help to guide me. Some brightness to alight the way.

I stumbled into the bark of another tree. Grimacing, I stand and search for the light of home; the candle I'd left glowing. Nothing but the soft glow of moonlight illuminating the trees could be seen. I was lost.
